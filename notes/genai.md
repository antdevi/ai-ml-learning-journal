# Introduction to Gen AI
AI --> ML --> Deep Learning --> Gen AI
## Gen AI: 
Capable of generating new datasuch as text, image, audio, video from scratch or based on existing data

## FMS
FMS are trained on internet scale unlabled data sets. They can perform a wide range of tasks because they contain a large no. of Parameter
Type of FMS: Text to text (Generative LLM), Text to embedding(compare inputs using embedding), Multi modal

## Prompt Engineering
Primary driver to get satisfactory results involving LLMs
Strategies of Prompting:
    Zero Shot Prompting: prompt won't contain examples or demonstration
    Few Shot Prompting: We teach AI models how to respond by showing them few examples of desired input output patterns
    Chain of thought Prompting: Enables complex resoning capabilities through intermediate reasoning steps
